High quality Eco-Packaging

To ensure that the products you buy and use are truly compostable, the Biodegradable Products Institute (BPI) created an innovative certification program in 1999. At Eco-Packaging, we offer wide variety of BPI certified products that met the American Society for Testing and Materials (ASTM) standards to certify products as true compostable.
